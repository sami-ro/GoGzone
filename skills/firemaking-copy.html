Firemaking

Firemaking is used to incinerate items for carbon and shards as well as making open fires using logs and matches (can be bought from the General Store for 2 coins a piece), which you then can cook fish over. When the fire goes out a pile of ashes will be left behind. Carbon, shards and ashes are used in Alchemy.
