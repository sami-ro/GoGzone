Alchemy

With alchemy you will create spells and teleports using clay pots, ashes and essence. Spells are used in Magic Combat while teleports instantly move you to a specific location. A certain level of Magic Focus is required to cast both.

Shards, which you turn into essence using a pestle & mortar (can be bought from the General Store for 4 coins), are obtained from incinerating items with Firemaking.

To make a pot of ashes simply combine ashes with a clay pot, this will provide 10 alchemy experience.

Shards Essence

To turn your shards into essence, simply use the shards with a pestle & mortar.
100 shards equal 1 essence.
