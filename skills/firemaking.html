Firemaking

Firemaking is used to incinerate items for carbon and shards as well as burning logs using matches (can be bought from the General Store for 2 coins a piece). You can cook fish over the open fires you start by burning logs and once the fire goes out a pile of ashes will be left behind.
Carbon, shards and ashes are used in Alchemy.

Incineration

There are twelve different shards you can get by incinerating items: Air, Water, Earth, Fire, Metal, Sharp, Force, Poison, Nature, Void, Bind and Soul. Shards are used to make essence with Alchemy. Experience gain is equal to amount of carbon you get from incinerating.
