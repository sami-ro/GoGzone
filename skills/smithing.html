Smithing

Smithing is used to make equipment out of ores gathered by Mining.

Smelting

Before you can make your ores into equipment you first need to smelt them into bars using a furnace.

Level Bar Type Materials Needed (quantity) Exp. Bar
1 Copper Copper Ore (2) 25
10 Iron Iron Ore (2) 50
20 Steel Iron Ore (2) + Coal (1) 75
30 Nelenite Nelenite Ore (2) + Coal (2) 100
40 Gothite Gothite Ore (2) + Coal (2) 125
50 Osmium Osmium (2) + Coal (3) 150

Smithing

Once you have bars and a hammer (can be bought from the General or Metalsmith Store for 5 coins) you can start making equipment. Simply use a bar on an anvil to bring up the list of equipment you can make. Experience gain depends on how many of which tier bar is used. Per used bar you will get experience equivalent of the experience you get for smelting a bar of that tier.
