Magic Combat

Magic Combat consists of three skills:
Magic Focus which determines what spells and teleports you can cast and your accuracy in battle.
Magic Power which determines how much damage you deal with spells.
Magic Defense which determines what tier armors you can equip and your defensive capabilites against magic attacks.

Experience is gained for each attack and is equal to damage dealt times ten. You pick which skill you want to train by selecting which type of attack you want to use in the combat menu.

Spells are made using Alchemy and some can be bought from vendors.
