Fletching

In fletching you will use a knife (can be bought from the General Store for 4 coins) to create bows and arrows using logs, bow strings (made with Crafting), feathers and arrowheads (made with Smithing).

Arrows

Arrows are created by making arrow shafts to which you then add a feather (dropped by chickens) and an arrow head. Arrow shafts don't have different tiers, but you will get a higher number of shafts from higher tier logs. Adding a feather to the arrow shaft grants 5 fletching experience while adding an arrowhead grants experience depending on the tier of the arrowheads.
