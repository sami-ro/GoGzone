Mage Equipment and Item Bags by using needles, thread and cloth.

Item Bags

Item bags can hold multiple non-stackable items while taking up only one inventory space. The items within a bag can be used normally like any other item that would be in your inventory. Item bags wear out over time and have to be repaired after a certain amount of times an item has been incerted in the bag. This number can be increased and has no upper limit, while the max number of items a bag can hold at once caps out at 100. Item bags are also bound to a certain item you choose yourself by using said item with the bag and cannot be changed later.
