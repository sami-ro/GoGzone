Construction

Construction is used to build camps and dummies as well as upgrading crafting stations. Camps and dummies are used to gather resources and train combat automatically while crafting station upgrades let you craft slightly faster.

The number of structures/upgrades you can have simultaneously depends on your number of adventurers and their construction levels. The number goes up by one for each adventurer you have unlocked and subsequently by one for each 20 levels in construction they collectively have.

Dummies have a set number of HP depending on their tier while camps have a number of uses depending on your construction level. Crafting station upgrades are permanent and will use up one structure slot as well. In order do build anything you will need a saw (can be bought from the General Store for 3 coins), logs and some items.
