Ranged Combat

Ranged combat consists of three skills:
Ranged Focus which determines what tier bows and arrows you can equip and your accuracy in battle.
Ranged Power which determines how much damage you deal with ranged weapons.
Ranged Defense which determines what tier armors you can equip and your defensive capabilites against ranged attacks.

Experience is gained for each attack and is equal to damage dealt times ten. You pick which skill you want to train by selecting which type of attack you want to use from the combat menu.

Bows and arrows are made using Fletching and some can be bought from vendors.
